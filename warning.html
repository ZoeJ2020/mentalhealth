Warning Signs

Mental disorders and difficulties with mental health usually build up over time from multiple factors. If you are experiencing a combination of these early warning signs, it may be useful to follow up with a medical professional.

- Sleep/Appetite changes

Dramatic changes in sleep or appetite such as overeating or oversleeping

- Mood swings

Rapid changes in emotion, particularly from one extreme to the other

- Loss of motivation

Apathy, lack of desire or initiative to perform usual daily activities such as cleaning or getting dressed

- Disconnection

Vague feeling of disconnection from other people, relationships and the world in general

- Concentration

Problems with concentration, memory or logical thinking that are difficult to explain
